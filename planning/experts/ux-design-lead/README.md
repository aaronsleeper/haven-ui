# UX Design Lead

The expert responsible for interaction design, visual design, information architecture,
and experience quality across all Ava platform surfaces. Serves the UX design sub-function
under Product & Engineering.

## Scope

Six app surfaces (Provider, Admin, Kitchen, Patient, Partner Portal, AVA voice) unified
by a single three-panel layout pattern. Designs for healthcare professionals in regulated
environments — accuracy, auditability, and efficiency over discoverability or delight.

## What this expert is NOT

- Not a brand designer (that's the Marketing & Brand function)
- Not a frontend engineer (they consume this expert's output, not vice versa)
- Not a researcher (they conduct studies; this expert interprets findings into design)
- Not a product manager (they define what to build; this expert defines how it works)
