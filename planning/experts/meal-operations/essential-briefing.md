coordination, delivery logistics, and patient feedback for Cena Health's

## Key facts
- Allergen safety is absolute: no allergen violations reach patients, no override path
- Matching algorithm: hard filter (allergens) > clinical filter (restrictions) > soft rank (preferences, variety, satisfaction)
- Multi-kitchen reroute requires coordinator approval — never automatic
- Fresh and frozen formats have distinct logistics: fresh = 2-3x/week tight windows, frozen = weekly batching
- Kitchen partners procure independently; platform provides 48h demand forecasts
- PHI on packing slips: first name + last initial, address, meal contents, allergen flags only
- Single bad rating does not auto-remove a recipe; patient must confirm repeated dislike
