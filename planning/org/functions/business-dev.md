# Business Development

> How Cena Health grows. Pipeline generation, partner acquisition, and market
> expansion. Relationship-driven on the front end with data-driven targeting.

---

## Responsibilities

- Market research and opportunity identification
- Lead generation and qualification
- Partner prospecting and outreach
- Proposal and RFP response
- Pricing and deal structuring
- Pipeline tracking and forecasting
- Market expansion planning (new geographies, populations, payers)
- Grant identification and application

## Sub-functions

| Sub-function | Automation target | Agent coverage | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Market research | 🤖 Automated | None (gap) | Competitive landscape, market sizing, trends |
| Lead identification | 🤝 Agent-assisted | None (gap) | Target health systems, MCOs, payers |
| Outreach | 🚫 Always human | CommunicationAgent (template support) | Relationship initiation requires human |
| Proposal/RFP response | 🤝 Agent-assisted | None (gap) | Agent drafts from templates + outcome data |
| Pricing/deal modeling | 🤝 Agent-assisted | None (gap) | See [OQ-41](../../open-questions.md) |
| Pipeline tracking | 🤖 Automated | None (gap) | CRM integration, stage tracking |
| Market expansion analysis | 🤝 Agent-assisted | ReportingAgent | Data-driven geographic/demographic analysis |
| Grant applications | 🤝 Agent-assisted | None (gap) | Agent drafts from templates; human customizes |

## Interfaces

| Direction | Function | What flows |
|---|---|---|
| **From** | Executive | Growth strategy, target markets |
| **From** | Data & Analytics | Outcomes data for proof points |
| **From** | Marketing & Brand | Content, materials, qualified leads |
| **To** | Partner & Payer | New partner agreements for onboarding |
| **To** | Executive | Pipeline status, market intelligence |
| **To** | Finance | Deal values, revenue projections |
| **To** | Legal & Corporate | Contract requests for new deals |
| **From** | Customer Success | Expansion opportunities, reference partners |

## Agent gaps

This function has the most agent gaps in the org chart. This is appropriate —
business development at early stage is founder-driven relationship work. As the
company scales, these sub-functions become automatable:

1. **First to automate:** Market research, pipeline tracking, competitive monitoring
2. **Second:** Proposal drafting, grant application support, pricing models
3. **Last:** Outreach and relationship management (always human-primary)
