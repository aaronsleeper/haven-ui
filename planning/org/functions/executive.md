# Executive & Strategy

> The company's brain — vision, fundraising, board management, strategic decisions.
> This function is structurally human. Agents inform but never decide.

---

## Responsibilities

- Set company direction, product strategy, and market positioning
- Fundraising — investor relations, pitch materials, financial projections
- Board management — updates, governance, compliance with corporate obligations
- Partner strategy — which health systems to pursue, pricing, contract terms
- Hiring decisions — who to bring on, when, at what level
- Resource allocation — where to invest time and capital across functions

## Quality checks

- Strategic decisions documented in [decisions.md](../../decisions.md) with rationale
- OKRs reviewed quarterly with measurable outcomes
- Board/investor materials reviewed by at least two founders before sending
- Partnership terms reviewed by counsel before signing

## Automation roadmap

**Phase 1 (near-term):** Agent-generated board reports and investor updates from live data.
Agent-drafted OKR progress summaries. Research briefs for strategic decisions.

**Phase 2 (mid-term):** Automated competitive intelligence monitoring. Pipeline forecasting
from CRM data. Financial scenario modeling for fundraising.

**Phase 3 (long-term):** Strategic planning support — agent surfaces patterns across all
company data to inform quarterly planning. Still human-decided, but better-informed.
