# Staff SOPs — DRAFTS (internal review)

Draft standard operating procedures for the UConn pilot (cap-64 + the enrollment process). Four drafts are here: the three staff role SOPs (Care Coordinator, Registered Dietitian, Administrator) plus the cross-role Participant Enrollment & Onboarding procedure.

**Note on the Admin SOP:** its task set is *proposed*, not settled — cap-64 left admin responsibilities TBD. Treat it as a draft to react to, and confirm the scope (Vanessa).

## Why this folder is labeled DRAFT

These are working drafts for internal review — not approved documents. Each SOP carries a visible **DRAFT** banner and a **pending sign-off** panel. They are **not approved for training and must not be used to guide care** until the sign-off gates are completed.

## Requirements to adhere to

- **No PHI, ever.** These documents must never contain patient data. They describe roles, workflows, and systems — never individual patient information. Any example must be synthetic / de-identified. This is precisely what lets them live and be reviewed here safely.
- **Internal only.** Do not forward outside Cena without reconciling first. In particular, the RD SOP flags the session-modality item (the RFP-committed all-in-person vs. the contract's remote allowance) that is **not yet reconciled with UConn** — keep that internal until it's resolved.
- **Comment, don't edit.** Give feedback as comments directly on the PDFs (Drive's PDF viewer supports this). The PDFs are *rendered exports* — the canonical source lives in the Haven design system and is regenerated, so edits to the PDF won't flow back.
