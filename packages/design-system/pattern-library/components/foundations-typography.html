Prose Section

Use .prose-section to constrain text content to a readable max width of 72ch. Ideal for articles, help text, and long-form content.

Medically tailored meals are a critical intervention for patients managing chronic conditions such as diabetes, heart failure, and chronic kidney disease. By delivering nutritionally appropriate meals directly to patients, care teams can improve adherence to dietary recommendations, reduce hospital readmissions, and support better long-term health outcomes.

The Cena Health platform streamlines this process by connecting care coordinators who assess patient needs, kitchens that prepare the meals, and delivery partners who ensure timely fulfillment. Each step is tracked, documented, and visible to every stakeholder in the chain of care.
