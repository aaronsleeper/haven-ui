Shadows

Brand note: Haven uses warm-tinted shadows based on rgba(78, 68, 55) (warm brown) instead of cool black. This is defined as --shadow-3 in defaults.css and ensures all elevations feel grounded in the brand's warm palette rather than appearing cold or sterile.
