Quantity Stepper

Patient-app +/- stepper for adjusting numeric quantities. Pill-shaped, secondary register. Behavior ships as src/scripts/components/quantity-stepper.js; consumers wire by including that file or by porting the contract to their framework.

Default (unbounded)

2

Bounded — data-min="0" data-max="10"

5

At minimum — auto-disables decrement (data-value="0" data-min="0")

0

At maximum — auto-disables increment (data-value="10" data-max="10")

10
