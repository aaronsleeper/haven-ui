Patient consent — meal-delivery program

By participating in this program, you agree to share with your care team the meals you order, your dietary preferences, and any health metrics you log voluntarily. Your care team uses this information to plan your meals and check in on your progress.

You can withdraw at any time by talking to your dietitian or your care coordinator. Your medical records and prescription benefits are not affected by your participation in the meal-delivery program.

UConn Health and its meal-delivery partners follow HIPAA and applicable state privacy law. Your information is shared only with the people on your care team and the kitchen partner preparing your meals.
