Relationships

  • Trigger → Decision: agent-to-agent within-lane flow (default arrow).
  • Decision → Clinical lead review: red/yellow severity routes to attestation gate (emphasized arrow).
  • Clinical lead review → Hand-off to BHN: signed approval routes the case to partner (emphasized arrow).
  • Hand-off → Audit record: acknowledgment closes the case to audit (default arrow).
  • Clinical lead review → Audit record: deferred or downgraded cases skip handoff and route directly to audit (muted dashed arrow).
  • Hand-off → Clinical lead review: ack-timeout pages the clinical lead back (muted dashed arrow).
