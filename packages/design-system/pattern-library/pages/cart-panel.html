Cart Panel

Canonical bridge — stepper → cart

Sibling cart-panel + menu-grid wired by a small bridge script (read the source of this page). The cart-panel does not auto-subscribe to stepper events — the consumer listens for quantity-change CustomEvents bubbling from the meal-option-card steppers and calls cartEl._cartPanel.setQty(mealId, qty) (or addItem when the meal isn't yet in the cart). Try adjusting quantities to watch the cart total, budget remaining, helper text, submit-disabled state, and the live-region announcement all update.
