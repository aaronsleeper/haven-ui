Weekly check-in call — Care Coordinator routine

DRAFT • v0.1 • pending Marrero + Healthcare Data Governance review

Cena's weekly check-in call flow runs hundreds of times across the 26-week intervention. The Care Coordinator selects a participant from the roster, conducts a 10–15 minute call covering order support, adherence, and barriers, classifies the outcome, and either logs and sets the next-touch plan, escalates to the UConn clinical team on a yellow flag, or schedules a follow-up retry for unsuccessful contact.

Elements

  • Trigger — Roster top by days-since-last-contact — agent enquiry step in the AGENT lane; semantics resolved.
  • Decision — Channel selection (phone or text) — agent decision step in the AGENT lane; channel-switch protocol pending cap-26.
  • Enquiry — 10–15 minute check-in — human enquiry step in the HUMAN lane, HIGH emphasis as the gravity center of the routine; cap-25 script and cap-26 protocol unauthored.
  • Decision — Outcome classification (5-way) with yellow-flag overlay — human decision step in the HUMAN lane, HIGH emphasis; yellow-flag criteria pending Marrero.
  • Action — Log outcome + set next-touch — human action step in the HUMAN lane closing the routine path; structured-field schema pending Marrero.
  • Hand-off — Yellow flag to UConn clinical team — cross-organization hand-off in the PARTNER lane, HIGH emphasis; receiving-actor identity (UConn-clinical-team vs. Cena-staffed BHN) unresolved (catalog #1 carry-forward); first-action specifics pending Vanessa/Marrero (catalog #2 carry-forward).
  • Action — Schedule follow-up retry — human action step in the HUMAN lane, LOW emphasis; cap-26 multi-modal contact protocol entirely undefined.

Relationships

  • Trigger → Channel selection: agent-to-agent within-lane flow (default arrow).
  • Channel selection → 10–15 minute check-in: cross-lane (AGENT → HUMAN) — channel is selected and the call is initiated (default arrow).
  • 10–15 minute check-in → Outcome classification: within-lane flow as the CC classifies at the end of the call (default arrow).
  • Outcome classification → Log outcome: successful contact (no yellow flag) routes to log-and-next-touch (default arrow).
  • Outcome classification → Yellow-flag hand-off: emphasized cross-lane arrow (HUMAN → PARTNER) marking the cross-org escape hatch.
  • Outcome classification → Follow-up retry: voicemail, no answer, refused, or deferred outcomes route to retry scheduling (muted dashed arrow).
  • Yellow-flag hand-off → Log outcome: ack received from UConn clinical team; CC logs the escalation and sets next-touch (default arrow).
  • Follow-up retry → Log outcome: retry attempt outcomes log back through the same action step (muted dashed back-edge).
