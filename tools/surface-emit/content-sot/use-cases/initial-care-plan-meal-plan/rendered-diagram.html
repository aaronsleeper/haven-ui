Initial care plan + meal plan — Month 0 combined-artifact creation

DRAFT • v0.1 • pending Marrero + Healthcare Data Governance review; SoP body reconciliation pending Vanessa

Cena's Month 0 combined-care-plan-and-meal-plan creation flow routes a participant from baseline-complete trigger through RD authoring, RDN approval, and hand-off to the downstream meal-prescription workflow. The RDN approval gate is enforced by the platform but is not yet surfaced in the RD SoP body — a structural documentation gap.

Elements

  • Trigger — All 6 baselines complete + Month 0 visit in progress — agent enquiry step in the AGENT lane; first-action specifics on visit start pending Vanessa/Marrero.
  • Action — RD authors combined care-plan + meal-plan artifact in Cena — human action step in the HUMAN lane; Dr. Wu's role on dietary requirements (review vs approve) pending Vanessa.
  • Attestation gate — RDN approval — human attestation in the HUMAN lane; the gate is hardcoded in the workflow-spec but is NOT surfaced in the RD SoP body as a named step — critical structural gap.
  • Hand-off — Activated artifact → downstream meal-prescription workflow — internal Cena hand-off in the SYSTEM lane; downstream workflow contract intentionally deferred (out of RD SoP scope).

Relationships

  • Trigger → Action: ready-to-author signal raised when baselines complete and visit is in progress (default arrow).
  • Action → RDN approval: completed draft routes to approval gate (emphasized arrow).
  • RDN approval → Hand-off: signed approval activates the artifact and triggers meal-prescription generation (emphasized arrow).
  • RDN approval → Action: revise decision routes the case back to authoring (muted dashed arrow).
  • Hand-off → RDN approval: downstream rejection pages the RDN (muted dashed arrow).

Four uncertainty annotations are surfaced visibly on the diagram: trigger first-action specifics (TBD pending Vanessa/Marrero), Dr. Wu role on dietary requirements (TBD pending Vanessa — review vs approve), RDN approval gate documentation (GAP CRITICAL — gate is in workflow-spec, not in SoP body), and downstream meal-prescription workflow contract (DEFERRED — out of RD SoP scope). The GAP CRITICAL annotation on the RDN approval gate is the single largest authoring-debt signal for this use case.
