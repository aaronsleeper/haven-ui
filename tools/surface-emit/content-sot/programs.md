## How a program joins the registry

A new program adds one row here and a new program space that reuses the UConn shape (overview · state ledger · critical path · timeline · capabilities · contract · decisions). The org level above does not change — that is the seam working as designed.

> Future programs slot in as sibling spaces. Until program #2 exists, the UConn pilot is the worked example of the shape.
