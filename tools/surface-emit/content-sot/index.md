## What Cena is

An agents-first health company: it pilots clinical-nutrition programs where AI agents do the throughput work and accountable humans (partner-institution clinicians) provide trust and the legal stop.

> **The thesis (org canon):** agents-first businesses outperform; humans provide accountability and trust, not throughput. Full identity, stage, and child entities: the `cena-health` entity doc (`Knowledge/Areas/Meta/Entities/cena-health/cena-health.md`) — canonical, referenced here, not restated.

## The org / program seam

What lives where — the rule that keeps this navigable as programs multiply:

- **Org-level (this level):** the company thesis, capabilities Cena offers across programs, the governance/compliance posture, and the program registry. Durable — it survives any single pilot.
- **Program-level (a child space):** a program's contract obligations, its capability mapping, its ops runbooks, evidence plan, timeline, and state ledger. Specific to that program; rolled up or archived when it completes.
- **The test:** adding "program #2" reuses the program shape with no restructuring. What you'd copy is program-level; what you'd share is org-level.
