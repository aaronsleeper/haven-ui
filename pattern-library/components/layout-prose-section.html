Program Description

The Diabetes Management Program provides medically tailored meals designed for patients with Type 2 diabetes. Each meal is developed in collaboration with registered dietitians and meets ADA nutritional guidelines for carbohydrate-controlled diets.

Enrolled patients receive 14 meals per week delivered to their home. Meal plans are adjusted monthly based on clinical data including HbA1c trends, weight changes, and patient-reported dietary preferences.

Care coordinators review adherence data weekly and escalate any missed-meal patterns to the clinical team for follow-up within 48 hours.
