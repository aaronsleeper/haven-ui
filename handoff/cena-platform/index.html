Cena Care-Coordinator Platform — walkthrough

Jump to any surface. The real app boots into Today (triage launchpad) and is navigated by the 7-anchor sidebar. Each link opens that surface's primary state; sibling states are linked from within. Demo data is synthetic.

The 7 anchors

Today — work queue

Triage launchpad: pushed alerts, pending referrals, order exceptions, cutoffs due. Deep-links into entities.

Patients

Roster, patient record (+ companion-app access), single & bulk intake.

Referrals

Partner-scoped pipeline → convert to patient.

Diet Operations

Providers · catalog · AI import · weekly plans · distribution · kitchen orders — one connected surface.

Network

Organization → program-partner graph; org & partner records.

Clinical Library

Dietary guidelines + diagnosis codes (reference).

Admin

Staff & roles · settings · EHR integrations.

This is the UX-designed replacement for the current engineer-designed care-coordinator platform. Spec lives in workflows/cena-platform/slots/; build backlog in cena-platform-pipeline-build.md. Pages land incrementally — a link to an unbuilt state may 404 until that surface ships.
